Tribute to Bhagat Singh

Bhagat Singh was an Indian revolutionary who played a crucial role in the Indian independence movement against British rule.

Some of his notable contributions include:

  • Participation in the Central Assembly Bombing
  • Formation of the Hindustan Socialist Republican Association
  • Advocacy for complete independence from British rule

If you have time, you should read more about this inspiring freedom fighter on his Wikipedia page.
